Personality

According to my most recent Jungian Archetype Test, I am a person with ESTJ type (extraversion, sensing, feeling and judging).

ESFJs are typically warm, empathetic, and sociable individuals who place a high value on tradition, loyalty, and cooperation. They are practical, detail-oriented, and seek harmony in their relationships with others. I think these descriptions are quite precise on me.

Hobbies

I have many hobbies: reading, eating, and running.

Reading

My favorite book is The Myth of Sisyphus (Le Mythe de Sisyphe) written by Camus. Camus tells a story that Sisyphus, a figure of Greek mythology who was condemned to repeat forever the same meaningless task of pushing a boulder up a mountain, only to see it roll down again just as it nears the top.

The reason why I love The Myth of Sisyphus is that I believe studying/doing research is just pushing a boulder up a mountain. No matter what the results will be, we should consistently keep our pace to do so.

Stay Perseverant and Stay Optimistic.
